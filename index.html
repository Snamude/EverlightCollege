Academics

Our curriculum is designed to empower students with both theoretical knowledge and practical skills that last a lifetime.

Sciences

Our science department focuses on practical experimentation and inquiry. We offer Biology, Chemistry, Physics, and Agriculture, ensuring students understand the natural world and modern agricultural techniques.

BIOLOGY CHEMISTRY PHYSICS

Humanities

Geography, History, and Christian Religious Education (C.R.E) are taught to give students a deep appreciation for our heritage, global dynamics, and spiritual foundation.

GEOGRAPHY HISTORY C.R.E

Languages & Math

Strong emphasis on English Language and Literature to enhance communication skills, paired with rigorous Mathematics to develop logic and analytical problem-solving abilities.

ENGLISH MATHS LITERATURE

Bakery & Cookery

Students learn the art of baking and food preparation. This practical course encourages creativity and equips them with marketable catering skills for the future.

Vocational Track

ICT & Computing

In the digital age, we ensure our students are computer literate. The course covers basic software usage, internet safety, and introduction to computer science.

Tech Track

Art & Tailoring

This track fosters artistic expression through garment design and tailoring. Students learn to create functional art, preparing them for the textile industry.

Creative Track

Code of Conduct

Building a Foundation of Character and Excellence that has the following essential qualities:

🙏

Devotion to God

We encourage students to cultivate a deep faith and seek to honor God in every aspect of their lives, making their beliefs the cornerstone of their journey.

📖

Academic Excellence

Students are challenged to reach their full potential and consistently strive for their personal best in all academic pursuits.

🛡️

Personal Integrity

We foster the courage to choose what is right, demonstrating honesty and ethical conduct regardless of the circumstances or challenges faced.

🌱

Environmental Stewardship

We instill a profound appreciation for nature and a commitment to preserving our environment for the well-being of future generations.
